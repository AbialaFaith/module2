The Obudu Plateau

The Obudu Plateau is found on the Oshie Ridge of the Sankwala Mountain range in Cross River State. It is a giant massive spread over an area of over 40 square miles and rises to about 5200 ft above sea level, with its peak reaching a height of about 1,716 metres above sea level. The plateau is known to be a habitat of rare species of birds.

Becheve Nature Reserve & Tree Nursery

The Becheve Nature Reserve is a conservation forest filled with various species of plants and animals. There are many species of trees and it holds some trees have been there before civilization.

Canopy Walkway

The Canopy Walkway is a 100m swinging bridge suspended above the forest, providing adventurers access to a forest canopy.
